Honda Motor Company is a Japanese car manufacturer that is one of the largest in the world. It is listed at #23 on Forbes Most Valuable Brands. This site will provide you info about Honda Motors and its products.

History

The company was founded in the year 1946 by Soichiro Honda. It produced its first product, an a-type bicycle engine in 1947. In 1959, Honda started its first overseas expansion in Los Angeles where it sold motorcycles. It later began to sell cars in 1969. In 1986, Honda launches Acura, the first Japanese luxury car brand. This leads to competitors like Toyota into releasing their own luxury car brand. In 1990, the Acura NSX is released as the first aluminum-bodied car. Honda releases the first gasoline-electric hybrid car in America in 1999.
