Honda Motor Company makes more than just cars, they produce motorcycles, power equipment, engines, jets, and more. They are the largest motorcycle manufacturer and engine manufacturer in the world.

Since 1986, Honda has invested in artificial intelligence and robotics research. They created their first robot called the ASIMO in 2000. ASIMO stands for Advanced Step in Innovative Mobility and was designed to help people such as the elderly. It can walk and dance and can even be controlled through a helmet using the wearer’s brainwaves. Since then, they have greatly improved upon its artificial intelligence. It can now run up to 5.6 miles per hour and can perform complex sign language. Honda will continue to improve the ASIMO in the future until it can be released.
